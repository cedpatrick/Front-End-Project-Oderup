ABOUT US

Oderup is an ode to secret recipes and cooking techniques in all Cuisines from across Undivided parts of the World. Founded by Ced Patrick in 1997 in Nosidia. Oderup serves up a flavourful melange of its signature multi-cuisine dishes, flavours and ingredients to deliver an unforgettable dining experience.



OUR STORY

During the Partition of Nosidia in 1992, Mr. Ced patrick migrated to Torablo from modern-day Laboshi. He always wanted to share with the city the culinary experience reminiscent of his years growing up near the North West Frontier Province. In November 1997, 5 years after he first landed in this new city, his love for the food took shape as the first Oderup in the heart of Nosidia. Every detail, from the even heating of custom clay ovens, to the creation of distinctive spice blends showcases the skills of our 'Ustads', our trained master chefs who were trained under Ced Patrick and have perfected over the years, the art of preparing and presenting dishes that are simply unforgettable. Thus we stand now with standard taste and Quality. Over the past 2 decades, Oderup has taken millions of guests on a culinary journey exploring and celebrating multiple cuisine, culture, and flavours.
